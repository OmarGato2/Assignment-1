Everything you need to know About Me.
"Optimism is the faith that leads to achievement. Nothing can be done without hope and confidence."

Hello! Welcome to my web page. My name is Omar and I am 19 years old. I was originally raised in Burnaby, but now I lived in Richmond to enjoy my life and adventure with my family. I live with my parents, my grandma, two brothers and two sisters.

My mom originally was planning for me to go to BC Muslim School, but I had a panic attack. So instead, I took Mitchell Elementary School and I enjoyed the subjects, field trips, friendships and other events that were memorable to me. I have gotten to know my friends students, who went to the same high school as me that I still keep in touch with as of now. I spended 8 years at Mitchell Elementary School since 2007.
