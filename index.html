Omar Gato

"Optimism is the faith that leads to achievement. Nothing can be done without hope and confidence."

Omar Iskandar Bekri Gato; born October 13, 2002)[1]is a Canadian student in British Columbia Institue of Technology. He was born and raised by Ikram Ibrahim and Iskandar Gato in Burnaby, British Columbia to ;Both Ikram and Iskandar are Ethiopian. Now, he resides in Richmond British, Columbia. He lives with his two brothers, Abdul and Ali, and two sisters, Nadia and Nazira, and his grandma, Bedriya.

He finished his education in Mitchell Elementary School, Cambie Secondary School, and Langara College. He is now enrolled in BCIT until 2026 based on the modified plan. His favourite role models are Will Smith and Michael Jackson. His current favourite artist is Dua Lipa.

Mitchell Elementary School (2007 - 2015)

"Working together to achieve our best." - Mitchell Elementary School

His mom, Ikram, originally was planning for him to go to BC Muslim School, but he had a panic attack. So instead, he took Mitchell Elementary School and enjoyed the subjects, field trips, friendships and other events that were memorable to him. He has gotten to know his friends, who he went to a similar high school. He still keeps in touch with them afterwards. He spended 8 years at Mitchell Elementary School and received a certificate at the Grad Assembly of 2015 since 2007.
